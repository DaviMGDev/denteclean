## Edge Cases

1. **Portuguese special characters** — Ensure proper UTF-8 encoding for accented characters (ã, ç, é, etc.)
2. **24-hour time format** — Brazil uses "8:00 às 18:00" format instead of AM/PM
3. **Brazilian phone format** — (DDD) 9XXXX-XXXX for mobile numbers
4. **LGPD vs HIPAA** — Brazil uses LGPD (Lei Geral de Proteção de Dados) instead of HIPAA
5. **CRO vs DDS** — Brazilian dental title is "CRO" (Conselho Regional de Odontologia)
